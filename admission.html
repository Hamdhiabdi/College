Admission Information

Welcome to our college admission page. We're thrilled that you're interested in applying to our college. We offer a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs, as well as certificate courses.

Application Process

To apply to our college, please follow the steps below:

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit your high school or college transcripts.
  3. Submit your standardized test scores, such as SAT or ACT.
  4. Write a personal statement.
  5. Pay the application fee.

Please note that some programs may require additional materials, such as a portfolio or letters of recommendation.

Important Dates

Our college operates on a rolling admission basis, which means we accept applications throughout the year. However, we recommend that you submit your application by the following dates:

  • Early Decision: November 1
  • Regular Decision: January 15
